SURGERY

We are well-trained in performing extensive surgeries even in declared inoperable cases. With our education, experience and training, we undertook a numerous surgery which were also successfully completed. From monitoring your vital signs during surgery, including to taking care of the patient after the surgery, we make sure his patients are as comfortable as possible while you recover.

CRYOTHERAPY

With the use of cryotherapy, we help in destroying a variety of benign skin growths in the patient's body such as pre-cancerous lesions (actinic keratoses), warts, and malignant lesions (basal cell and squamous). We have tackled stage 1V and inoperable cases by performing successful Cryotherapy treatments.

PROTECTED CHEMOTHERAPY

With the help of state-oof-art medic facilities and knowledge, we have successfully performed safe chemotherapy with the least side effect. As per the required treatment by the patient, we decide the type of drug needed to treat the cancer and with the gradual process, we kill most of the cancer cells from the patient’s body

IMMUNOTHERAPY

By using both chemical and cellular immune therapy concepts, we are skilled in performing Immunotherapy and treat patient’s immune system to fight cancer

RADIO FREQUENCY THERAPY

This minimal invasive treatment therapy is performed using the electrical energy and heat to destroy the cancer cells. Our trained radiologist perform this treatment to make your primary treatment more effective.

OZONE

With our skills and expertise, we perform Autohemotherapy and Intralesional Ozone installation treatment by injecting medical grade ozone gas into blood drawn from a patient. This is process is performed so that the ozone is mixed with the blood for a period of time and then later intravenously is infused back into the same patient. Since this treatment has a cumulative effect, it enhances the effects of previous treatments

HYPERTHERMIC CHEMOTHERAPY

Hyperthermic intraperitoneal chemotherapy (HIPEC) and Cytoreductive surgery (CRS) are combined together to treat patients with peritoneal carcinomatosis.

INTRA PERITONEAL, INTRAPLEURAL

Intralesional with combined Activated Charcoal + Chemo medicine Combo- Our doctors are highly skilled to perform this new technique of administering chemo in local areas without involving the general treatment system. Besides the above treatments, Dr. Chandra is adept in utilizing microprobes for dealing with liver cancer, thyroid cancers, etc with ultrasound guidance and using Cryo freezing or ethanol ablation techniques. He is trained in utilizing medicines and rare herbs noted in ancient literature to fight cancer and other so-called incurable diseases. He also utilize Natural killer cells (Stem cell variety) in the fight against cancer and also provide Acupuncture therapy in the fight against cancer, besides using it as the main treatment modality in the management of kidney failure, liver failure, ischemic heart disease, etc circumventing the need for haemodialysis, kidney transplant, liver transplant, open heart bypass surgeries stenting procedures, etc and in doing skin grafting as a day care procedure.

MISSION

Staying rooted to our traditions, we strive to provide the highest possible standard of patient care and treatment in an ethical and compassionate environment



VISION AND VALUES

To build a first-class patient-focused service based on high quality and evidence-based practice

To provide this service in an ethical, well-managed and considerate environment.

To promote a culture that will ensure high quality patient care and service

To ensure that decisions regarding delivery of service are patient focused and evidence based.

To support and invest in education and training, thereby encouraging the continuous development of hospital staff.e

MISSION STATEMENT



● Continuously improve all our services through quality management and research
● Focus on our patients and deliver high quality service
● Promise and deliver hope, cure and care
● Involve all our partners in our quality improvement activities
● Empower staff to make appropriate decisions
● Value diversity and have the highest degree of respect for one another
● Use training, teamwork and open communication to enable staff to achieve their full potential
● Recognise and reward staff' contributions
● Take all reasonable steps to ensure that patients, visitors, staff and all others in contact with the hospital are afforded the safest possible environment
● Maintain the highest ethical standards in protecting the public and the environment
● Measure the effectiveness of our activities and monitor progress towards achieving our vision
● Deliver high quality, safe and reliable healthcare in accordance with evidence based best practice in an effectively managed and maintained environment
● Provide healthcare based on the assessed needs and preferences of our patients which ensures mutual respect, holistic care and continuous learning and training
● Monitor, evaluate and continually improve our services
